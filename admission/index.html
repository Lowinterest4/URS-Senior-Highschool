Admission Process

Join our community of learners and start your journey to success. Our admission process is designed to be simple and straightforward

1

Submit Application

Complete and submit the online application form with all required information

2

Submit Documents

Provide all necessary documents including transcripts and certificates

3

Assessment & Interview

Attend the entrance assessment and interview with our admissions team

4

Enrollment

Receive acceptance letter and complete the enrollment process

Admission Requirements

Make sure to prepare these documents before starting your application

Completed Application Form (available online or at the school office)

Original and photocopy of Junior High School Report Card (Form 138)

Certificate of Good Moral Character from previous school

2 pieces of 2x2 ID photos (recent, white background)

Photocopy of Birth Certificate (PSA issued)

Parents or Guardian must accompany the applicant during enrollment
