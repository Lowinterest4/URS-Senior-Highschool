Founded in 1952, our senior high school has been a cornerstone of academic excellence and community development for over seven decades. What began as a small institution with just 150 students and 12 dedicated teachers has grown into a thriving educational center serving over 2,000 students annually.

Throughout the years, we have maintained our commitment to providing quality education while adapting to the evolving needs of our students and society. Our campus has expanded significantly, now featuring state-of-the-art science laboratories, a modern library with over 50,000 volumes, comprehensive sports facilities, and technology-integrated classrooms.

We have produced countless graduates who have gone on to become leaders in various fields including medicine, engineering, education, business, and public service. Our alumni network spans the globe, and many return to contribute to the continued growth and success of their alma mater.

The school has received numerous accolades over the years, including recognition for academic excellence, sports achievements, and community engagement. We remain dedicated to our founding principles of integrity, excellence, and service while embracing innovation and progress.
