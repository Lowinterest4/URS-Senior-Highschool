About URS Senior Highschool

We provide quality education that prepares students for college and career success. Our dedicated faculty and modern facilities create an environment where students thrive academically, socially, and personally.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements?

+

Students must submit their Form 138 (Report Card), birth certificate, and complete the entrance examination. Additional requirements may vary depending on the chosen strand.

What academic strands do you offer?

+

We offer STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics), ABM (Accountancy, Business, Management), and HUMSS (Humanities and Social Sciences) strands to match your career goals.

When does the school year start?

+

The school year typically begins in August and follows the academic calendar set by the Department of Education. Enrollment periods are announced several months in advance.

Do you offer scholarships?

+

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships and financial assistance programs for qualified students. Please contact our admissions office for more information about eligibility and application procedures.
